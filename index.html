WHY CHOOSE FRENCH JEWRNEY?

FRENCH JEWRNEY IS AN ENRICHING EXPERIENCE, A MEMORABLE JOURNEY IN THE LIVES OF ALL OUR PARTICIPANTS.

The stays organized by French Jewrney are high-end stays. They have the particularity and advantage of combining cultural discoveries and intensive learning of English. The programs are therefore very comprehensive, with English lessons in the morning, visits/activities in the afternoon and evenings organized by teams of qualified activity leaders. A concept that has proven itself in previous years. Immersing yourself in a pleasant English-speaking atmosphere gives young people the desire to learn and practice the language. This is a unique, exciting and very rewarding experience. LEARN MORE

Expert & leader in language stays for students in the USA since 2005, French Jewrney advises and supports you throughout your project, whatever it may be. With a dual objective: learning English and discovering foreign cultures.
